Deng Cai is a PhD student (final-year) at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Wai Lam. His research interests lie within natural language processing and machine learning. Particularly, his research centers on building symbolic representation and reasoning, and explicit and external memory. He has published research papers at prestigious conferences and journals, such as ACL, EMNLP, NAACL, and AAAI. He won an Outstanding Paper Award at ACL 2021. He served as program committee member in leading NLP conferences including ACL, EMNLP, NAACL etc, and was selected as an outstanding reviewer at EMNLP 2020. 

Website: https://jcyk.github.io/
